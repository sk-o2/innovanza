Gaming Maniac

    June 20, 2021     Venue - IIC Hall

“Be the last one standing!”

What is Gaming Maniac?

It is basically a fun event. Participants need to compete in two online games, namely Mini Militia and COD-Mobile and the winner shall be decided according to the criteria as specified further.


Flow of Event

  • Students from any school (9th to 12th standard),recognized institution or college can participate in this event.
  • It is basically a fun event. Participants need to compete in two online games, namely Mini Militia and BGMI and the winner shall be decided according to the criteria as specified further.
  • Participants first need to register for the event and they will be informed about the events and rules. Participation fee would be applicable for this event.
  • First Top 100 participants will selected after they compete in a match of Mini Militia. Those 100 participants will then be required to make a team of 4 among themselves. These 25 teams will be required to participate in a COD Battle Royale. Top 5 teams will be selected and then 20 participants will be required to make 4 teams of 5 players each. The semi-final and final will eventually be done in a TDM.
  • Top 3 among the final 4 teams left will be declared as winner and runner ups respectively.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • No external equipment is allowed.
  • Use of cheats/plug-ins is prohibited.
  • Camping is not allowed.
  • Decision of jury shall be final.
