Motor Vi

    June 20, 2021     Venue - IIC Hall

“Don’t worry about failure, you only have to be right once.”

What is DC Motor?

Motors are one of the most common yet important devices used in today's world. DC Motor Making, as the name suggests, requires participants to make a DC Motor with the specified parameters as they get to learn about the basic principles of a motor.


Flow of Event

  • >Before the main event a briefing will be provided to the students about the event, so the participants get know well about event.
  • After the briefing the registration form will be circulated.
  • The participants need to form a team of minimum 3 and maximum 4 students.
  • The team will be asked to make their DC motor as per the instructions given by their coordinators.
  • On the day of competition, the teams will be asked to present their DC motors.
  • The team with highest rpm of motor (measured by tachometer) will be the winner.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Each team can have a maximum of 3 members.
  • Participants are not allowed to use their cell phones during the event.
  • All participants should maintain discipline and follow the given instructions.
  • The decision of jury will be final. No objection regarding results shall be entertained.
  • otoStudents from any recognized institution can participate.
  • All participants need to carry their institute identity cards during the event.
  • Use of any flammable materials is restricted.
