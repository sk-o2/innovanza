Robo Fight

    June 20, 2021     Venue - IIC Hall

“Bring your bot and lets Fight”

What is Robo Fight?

Robo fight is a fun and exciting event to create and promote a culture of innovation among budding talents among the students of the institute. The challenge is to build your own robot, either wired or wireless within the specified range.


Flow of Event

  • Undergraduate college students from any Indian college, with knowledge of basic electronic components like motoranddrivingskillcanparticipate.
  • The participant will be briefed about the event and rules. They will also be given some basic ideas about the ring and about what components they can use and about point systems.
  • The participant will form a team of minimum 2 and maximum 4 members. One will be a controller and rest will the be supporter.
  • The participant will be shown the fighting ring on which they need to fight.
  • Finally, the fight will be conducted, it will be one at a time. Scoring will be done on the basis of our rating system and the decision of jury will be final.
  • Prize will be awarded to top 3 winners.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Teams must have a proper documentation of Robo containing a brief description about parts and working.
  • Once out of the arena, the robo will be eliminated.
  • Robo should be made according to the rules and regulations otherwise it will lead to disqualification.
  • The Robot must not damage the arena.
  • Each team must be given a name as chosen by its members. Team names must not be connected in any way with the names of companies or registered trademarks or use expressions that incite violence or are discriminatory, obscene, or represent any form of defamation. Failure to comply will result in disqualification.
  • Unethical behavior may lead to disqualification.
  • Administrator reserves the right in their sole discretion to disqualify any individual or maker it finds to be actually or presenting the appearance of tampering with the entry process or the operation of the event or to be acting in violation of these Official Rules or in a manner that is inappropriate, unsportsmanlike, not in the best interests of this Event, or a violation of any applicable law or regulation.

    • Robo Specifications:
    • The dimensions ofthe robot should be between 25cm x 20cm x 15cm (LxBxH) and 30cm x 25cm x20cm (LxBxH).
    • The Robo may be wired or wireless.
    • The length of the wire (for wired bots) should be about 1.5 to 2.5m and the wire should remain slack during the complete event.
    • Maximum weight must not exceed 3 kg.
    • Participants should be able to briefly describe the components used and its working.
    • Ifcheating or a ready-made it is found,such a machine will be disqualified.
