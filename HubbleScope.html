Hold the clusters of the stars between the centre of the optic.

What is Hubble Scope?

From the very start, we have a curiosity to see stars, constellations, other planets, or more things that are so far from us. For that curiosity we make telescopes. We also see in the movie of pirates and space documentaries how much the telescope used or necessities of telescopes. So we organize a event for making a telescope which is named as ‘Hubble Scope’ .


Flow of Event

  • It is an event in which a participant will make a telescope for seeing distant objects with the use of the concept of the telescope in class 12th. .
  • It is a team event.
  • It is a single-round event in which the alphabet which prints on a board standing in distance and the flow of the event happens as an eye test.
  • Participants will be making team comprising 4 -5 members.
  • The prize will be distributed to the top 3 participants and all others who will be complete the task will also be awarded..

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Undergraduate college students from any Indian college, with knowledge in optics can participate.
  • It is a team event or a single-man event. Every team should have a maximum of 4 members..
  • The telescope sliding/Fixed telescope for adjusting the focus.
  • Participants can make reflecting or refracting telescopes.
  • Participants can make their own lenses or buy a lens of a dimension.
    An objective convex lens of diameter 75mm
    Eyepiece lens of diameter 7.5mm
    https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yntyRTo-94A (link for the making of telescope)

Judging Criteria

  • The telescope which will used for seeing maximum distant letters declared as a winner.
  • The winners of the event decided by Jury Members and any objection by participants leads to a their disqualification.
