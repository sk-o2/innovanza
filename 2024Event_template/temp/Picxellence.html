Pixcellence

What it’s all about.

Any technical event feels incomplete without some splendid clicks to cherish for a lifetime. Picxellence is such an event where you get to unleash your shutter skills and compete among the budding photographers of the institute. This event shall be included as a competitive and fun event.

Event Guidelines

Participants are required to click pictures during the events of Innovanza’24. They then need to submit their entries via the Google form provided and the winning entries shall be finalised by the jury.

Rules and Regulations

1.Participants need to submit their entries via the given Google form.

2.A total of 7 entries should be submitted in .jpg format.

3.Use of templates/adding elements to image is not allowed.

4.Basic editing and enhancements are allowed.

5.Pictures clicked other than during Innovanza’24 shall be disqualified.

6.A total of seven entries are to be submitted on the last day of Innovanza through the Google form.

7.Use of photography gears such as DSLRs/any other gadgets is allowed.

Judging Criteria

1. The submitted entries shall be judged on the basis of creativity Picture composition Photography skills relevance to theme and overall appeal.
