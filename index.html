What is Innovanza ?

Innovanza is the science and technology festival of Bundelkhand Institute of Engineering and Technology. We aspire to reach out to college students across the globe to spark their interest in Science and Technology and provide them a platform to pursue the same.

Tech Events Fun Events

Great Speakers

Networking

The rich alumni network of the college provides an opportunity to the students to expand their network.

New People

Students get to engage with new people across the country by means of workshops and conferences

Have Fun

Students get the opportunity to participate in plethora of fun events including Open Mic and DJ Night
