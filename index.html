What is Innovanza ?

Innovanza is the science and technology festival of Bundelkhand Institute of Engineering and Technology.Techfest aspire to reach out to college students across the globe to spark their interest in Science and Technology and provide them a platform to pursue the same.

  • Inaguration Ceremony

    • By Engg. Ashish Patel Minister for Technical Education Government of UP
    • 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM

    Innovanza, our annual Techfest, is set to begin with an Inauguration Ceremony. The event will be graced by the presence of the Education Minister of State, who will officially kick off the festival. The festival will feature a variety of events and activities showcasing the latest advancements in technology and innovation, as well as the talents and ideas of students from across the state. Attendees can expect to engage with cutting-edge technology, learn from industry experts, and be inspired to make their own contributions to the field. Join us at Innovanza and be a part of the celebration of innovation and technology.

    Location: Innovation & Incubation Center, 1st floor Central Library, BIET Jhansi
    View Details

  • StartUP Mela

    • 100+ Startups Showcasing Innovative Products and Services
    • 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM

    StartUP Mela, a premier meetup and exhibition of startups, is set to take place. The event will feature more than 100 startups from the state showcasing their innovative products and services. Attendees can expect to engage with a wide range of startups across various industries, learn about the latest trends and technologies, and connect with industry leaders. The event will also feature keynote speeches, panel discussions and networking opportunities to help entrepreneurs and startups learn, grow and connect with potential investors, partners and customers. Join us at StartUP Mela to discover the next big thing in the startup world and be a part of the entrepreneurial ecosystem.

    Location: Innovation & Incubation Center, 1st floor Central Library, BIET Jhansi
    View Details

It requires a mix of knowledge, patience and focus in order to win.
