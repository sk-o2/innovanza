“Save your eggs from the fall!”

What is Pixcellence?

Any technical event feels incomplete without some splendid clicks to cherish for a lifetime. Photography is such an event where you get to unleash your shutter skills and compete among the budding photographers of the institute. This event shall be included as a competitive and fun event.


Flow of Event

  • Participants are required to click pictures during Innovanza events or on a specific theme told to them. They then need to submit their entries and the winning entries shall be finalised by the jury

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Participants need to submit their entries via a Google form.
  • A screenshot of EXIF also needs to be submitted.
  • Use of templates/adding elements to image is not allowed.
