Open Mic

    June 20, 2021     Venue - IIC Hall

“If you are the possessor of vocal arts the show why you are?"

What is Open Mic?

During Innovanza, we know that the daytime is full of pressure, competition and hecticness. In order to lighten up the mood and make the environment soothing we have a fun event ‘Open Mic’. Everyone has some hidden talent which is other than their technical knowledge and this event is for them to exhibit their talent in front of the whole institute. Participants get to choose from a wide range of activities such as singing, poetry, stand-up comedy, dance, mimicry and many more. Open Mic will be organized every night during the entire duration of the Innovanza tech fest.


Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Participants need to register and mention their talents.
  • Participants can perform either solo or in groups.
  • Vulgar and obscene performances are not allowed.
