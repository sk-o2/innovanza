Action Cannon

    June 20, 2021 Venue - IIC Hall

An idea that is not dangerous is unworthy of being called an idea at all.

What is Canon Launcher?

Well, every child has a dream to use cannon and gun at some point of their life. This event is being organized in order to bring out that child with new perspective. This event of cannon making is a test to all the theoretical knowledge that we have gained.


Flow of Event

  • The Participants will be briefed about the event and rules. They will also be given some basic ideas about cannon and about what components they can use.If requiredthey will also be trained about Cannon making and designing skills.
  • The Participants will form a team of minimum 3 and maximum 5 and registerfor the event. They also need to prepare a document about components used. Participating groups have to bring their functional cannon on the day of competition.
  • The final round will be conducted, it will be one at a time, having three chances for each group. Scoring will be done on the basis of our rating system and the decision of jury will be final.
  • Prize will be awarded to top 3 participants.

Rules and Regulations of the event

  • Undergraduate college students from any Indian college, with knowledge of basic mechanical components can participate.
  • A team must contain a minimum of 3 to maximum of 5 members. Only one of them will launch the canon ball and the rest will support him.
  • No inflammable material will be used otherwise the team will be eliminated.
  • Each team must be given a name as chosen by its members. Team names must not be connected in any way with the names of companies or registered trademarks or use expressions that incite violence or are discriminatory, obscene, or represent any form of defamation. Failure to comply will result in disqualification.
  • Unethical behavior may lead to disqualification.
  • Administrator reserves the right in their sole discretion to disqualify any individual or maker it finds to be actually or presenting the appearance of tampering with the entry process or the operation of the event or to be acting in violation of these Official Rules or in a manner that is inappropriate, unsportsmanlike, not in the best interests of this Event, or a violation of any applicable law or regulation.

Judging Criteria

  • In first round all groups with working cannon will be selected.
  • Group having the longest cannon range will be declared the winner.
  • The decision of the judges will be absolute and who will question it will be disqualified.
